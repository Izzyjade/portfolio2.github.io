Town Hall Posters

Throughout my time here at Olin, I have made posters for many different event posters, whether it be for Student Affairs, The Library, or the Honor Board. This page highlights posters from an event that I am very proud of. One of my activities at Olin is being on the Honor Board, an organization part of the student government that oversees the honor code and hears cases. In the spring of 2016, we spent most of our time rewriting the honor code to be more condensed and readable, along with some content changes. In order for these changes to take affect, they had to be voted on by more than 50% of the student body at a town hall meeting.

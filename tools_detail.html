Library Tool Wall

I work in the library here at Olin, and it is not like a normal college library. Over the past 3 years, it has been transformed from a rarely used research library into the hub of campus. Over the past year, I have been working in the library to continue this transformation.

One of my favorite projects has been the Tool Wall. Previously the tools were all on standard library shelves, and you couldnt identify what was missing or where things were.We aimed to solve this problem with a new slant shelf design, that slots into a redesigned kerf wall that has been modified to mount to the standards. We also vinyl cut outlines of the tools, so you always know where to put it back, and can tell if it is missing.

I worked on this project with three other students. My role in this has been helping with the original design and fabrication, creating and cutting the tool outlines, and continuing to improve and maintain the wall.
